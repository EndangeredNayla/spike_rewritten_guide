Gardening

Gardening in Toontown is a great and easy way to earn an additional 4 laff points! Any toon, at any level, can start their own garden with the gardening kit at their estate. The gardening kit is purchasable in Clarabelle's Cattlelog. Once received, a gardening guide will be shown, then tree plots and gardening trays will assemble itself around a toons estate.

How to Plant Flowers

To start planting flower, simply walk up to one of the 10 avaliable flower planting spots surrounding the toon estate and click "Plant Flower". Upon clicking that, the jellybean choser will pop up and a toon will have to put in a correct flower jellybean combination to successfully plant a flower. Remember to water all of the flowers planted, or they will not be avaliable to harvest the next day. All plants that were planted and watered before midnight will grow at 1:00 AM Pacific time. To sell flowers, approach the wheel barrow at the estate. Flowers sell for double the plant cost. Click here to check out all of the jellybean flower combinations!

Similar to fishing, with every 10 new species of flowers discovered, a laff point is earned! There are a total of 40 flower species and 4 earnable gardening trophies.

Shovels & Watering Cans

To discover new flower species, toons must advance their shovel. There are currently four different shovels that can be used to garden. To advance the shovel, flowers must be planted and watered at the highest level (jellybean cost) and harvested each day, to earn up to a maximum of 10 points a day. The following chart is a great source to use to know when a shovel will upgrade and when laff points can be earned:

Shovel Points to Advance Shovel Level Flower Jellybean Cost Laff Point?
Tin 0 - 39 1 1 jellybean No
Tin 40 - 79 2 2 jellybeans Yes
Bronze 0 - 79 1 3 jellybeans No
Bronze 80 - 159 2 4 jellybeans Yes
Silver 0 - 159 1 5 jellybeans No
Silver 160 - 319 2 6 jellybeans Yes
Gold 0 - 319 1 7 jellybeans No
Gold 320 - 639 2 8 jellybeans Yes

Along with shovels, there are four different water cans a toon can earn and use to garden. To upgrade, a toon must earn skill points by watering a plants a specific amount of times. The following chart will to use to know when a watering can will be upgraded and what characteristics each can has:

Watering Can Points to Upgrade Days Lasted per Water
Small 0 2 - 4 days
Medium 100 6 - 8 days
Large 200 10 - 12 days
Huge 400 14 - 16 days

Gag Trees

In addition to planting flowers, toons can also plant gag trees with, well, gags! Once fully grown, that gag will gain what is called an "organic bonus", which increases gag damage by 10% (rounded up), or increases accuracy for lure gags. There are a total of eight avaliable spots per toon to plant a gag tree. However, to have an organic bonus on any level 2+ gag, the preceding gag in that track must also be planted and that lower gag tree must not be removed.

Note, the higher the gag level planted, the longer it will take to grow the gag tree. A good rule of thumb to use is multiply the gag level by 3 and that is the number of days it will take the gag tree to fully grow.

Statues

Stautes are a great way of making a toons garden look complete and fancy. What more could you want! There are two types of statues: Disney character and special stautes. To plant a statue, a toon must buy it in Clarabelle's Cattlelog and then approach the silver plot outside the estate to set it up. At any level, a special statue can be planted. However, the Disney themed statues have to be earned. Here is a quick guide to the Disney statues:

  • Donald Statue → have the 2 jellybean shovel
  • Mickey Statue → have the 4 jellybean shovel
  • Minnie Fountain → have the 6 jellybean shovel
  • Mickey Fountain → have the 8 jellybean shovel
  • Toon Statues → max out the shovel track
