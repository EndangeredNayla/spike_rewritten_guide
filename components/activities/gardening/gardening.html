Gardening

Gardening in Toontown is a great and easy way to earn an additional 4 laff points! Any toon, at any level, can start their own garden with the gardening kit at their estate. The gardening kit is purchasable in Clarabelle's Cattlelog.

Planting Flowers

Similar to fishing, with every 10 new species of flowers discovered, a laff point is earned! There are a total of 40 flower species and 4 earnable gardening trophies.

To discover new flower species, toons must advance their shovel. There are currently four different shovels that can be used to garden. To advance the shovel, flowers must be planted and watered at the highest level (jellybean cost) and harvested each day, to earn up to a maximum of 10 points a day. To sell flowers, approach the wheel barrow at the estate. Flowers sell for double the plant cost. The following chart is a great source to use to know when a shovel will upgrade and when laff points can be earned:

Gag Trees

In addition to planting flowers, toons can also plant gag trees with, well, gags! Once fully gorwn, that gag will gain what is called an "organic bonus", which increases gag damage by 10% (rounded up), or increases accuracy for lure gags. There are a total of eight abaliable spots per toon to plant a gag tree. However, to have an organic bonus on any level 2+ gag, the preceding gag in that track must also be planted and that lower gag tree must not be removed.
